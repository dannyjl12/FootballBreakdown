Welcome to FootballBreakdown.com, your complete breakdown of different football clubs, players and tactics from across the world! When evaluated, each club will be given a full historical breakdown from when they were founded to present day, including their club philosophy, achievements, club legends, and more! There will also be information on the roster, their key players, and youth prospects to watch out for in the future.

Every player that is evaluated will be given a full biography as well as a complete analysis of their style of play, transfer history, strengths, and weaknesses.

Every week, a new club or player will be evaluated. For any clubs or players that you would like to see, you can make suggestions here!
